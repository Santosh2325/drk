Facilities
Classrooms
All classrooms are AC with a laptop, LCD, sound system attached having U-shape seating arrangements and 29” TV and a DVD player.
Library
6000 books and more than 100 journals and newspapers are subscribed. All latest books brought from Europe, USA and Canada. Library works between 8.00 am to 9.00 pm everyday including holidays. 4000 journals on electronic mail available for reference.
Mess
Subsidized run by a contractor and managed by a student committee. They decide the menu; ensure quality food, service and budget.
Canteen
Subsidized canteen managed by a student committee run by a contractor, quality stuff and service to be ensured by the representative student committee. Night services run by students.
Guest room
"Guest room is available in the campus at the rate of Rs. 300 per day"
Medical Insurance and Clinic
All students insured. A Panel Doctor, Dr.(Mrs.) Kalyanshetty at NRI complex Seawood is available for consulting. Visiting campus 2 days for 2 hours. Panel Clinic in case of hospitalization.
Hostel rooms
The institute will provide every student with a cot, mattress, small almirah, study table and chair. Students will bring their own Toiletteries, pillow, bedsheets, bucket, mug and mosquito net.
Common room in the institute
Has 2 nos. 34” TV, newspapers, sofas and wireless net facility – open 24 hours a day. To be managed by a student committee. Audio system available in the common room.
Student Emergency Loan
All students contribute Rs.50/- per month to this fund and are entitled to returnable emergency loans. The fund to be managed by a faculty and a student committee.
Student sponsorships in India
All expenses are refunded by the institute when a student is sponsored to attend events within Mumbai, in India or abroad for a project presentation.
Work Experience Opportunities
Institute has arrangements with BPOs, Retail Chains, Banking Institutions, Manufacturing industry for paid-on-the-job training. This enables them to have exposure to real life working environments and enhance their experience.
Internet Lab
Fully equipped wireless network. Works from 8.00 am to 10.00 pm everyday including holidays.
Computer Lab
Fully equipped wireless network. Works from 8.00 am to 10.00 pm everyday including holidays.
Bank
Bank of Maharashtra, is available in DRK Campus. With 24*7 ATM Open in Campus. Education Loan, Scholership Accounts of students are handled by the Bank.
Transportation
For field visits and corporate meetings institute car available to students.
Executive Dress
A Shirt, Necktie, a Blazer, Trouser and a T-Shirt
Study Material
We provide study material to all the students.
